🎮 About The Game
Subway Hoppers is a thrilling fast-paced 3D endless runner game built entirely with web technologies. Set in the vibrant and chaotic world of Indian Railways, you play as a runner dashing through the tracks of a busy Indian city — dodging massive trains, leaping over barriers, sliding under electric wires, and collecting shiny rupee coins — all while being relentlessly chased by a determined mustachioed cop!
The game draws heavy inspiration from the colorful culture of India — featuring auto rickshaws, ancient temples, palm trees, street food stalls, festival flags, and the iconic sounds and sights of a busy Indian railway station. Every run feels alive, vibrant, and uniquely Indian.
Whether you're a casual player looking for a quick fun session or a competitive runner chasing the highest score — Subway Hoppers has something for everyone!

🎯 Gameplay
You start as a runner on the railway tracks of a busy Indian city. The city never stops — buildings blur past, auto rickshaws honk on the sides, temple domes glitter in the golden light, and festival flags flutter overhead.
Your goal is simple — run as far as you can without getting caught or hit.
The further you run, the faster the game gets. Obstacles come at you quicker, the cop closes in, and only the sharpest reflexes will keep you alive. Collect as many rupee coins as you can along the way to unlock new characters in the shop.
How to survive:

Switch lanes to avoid obstacles
Jump over low barriers and crates
Slide under electric wires and high obstacles
Grab power-ups to get an edge
Collect coins to unlock characters

🚧 Obstacles
The tracks are never clear — here's what stands between you and glory:
🚂 Train
The biggest and most terrifying obstacle. A massive orange Indian Railways train blocks the entire lane. You cannot jump over it — you must switch lanes entirely or use Fire Power to blast through!
🚧 Barrier
A classic red and white railway barrier blocks your path at low height. Jump over it to survive. If you're sliding, you'll crash right into it!
📦 Crate
Heavy wooden cargo crates stacked on the tracks. Jump over them to get past. They're solid — no sliding under these!
⚡ Electric Wire
High tension wires strung between poles across the track. You need to slide under them — jumping will get you electrocuted! These are the trickiest obstacles to read.

⚡ Power-Ups
Scattered rarely across the tracks are two incredibly powerful items that can completely change your run:
🔥 Fire Power
Pick up the blazing fire orb and become UNSTOPPABLE! For a limited time:

All obstacles on the track are instantly destroyed
Your character glows with intense fire
The screen turns a dramatic fiery orange
No obstacle can stop you — just run and collect coins!

💨 Bean Power
Pick up the mysterious brown bean and get an INCREDIBLE SUPER JUMP! For a limited time:

Your jump height is doubled
You can reach coins that are normally too high
Cloud particles burst out with every jump
Your character floats with a bouncy, magical energy
Perfect for collecting hard-to-reach coins!

🪙 Coin System
Coins are the lifeblood of Subway Hoppers. Here's how they work:

Coins appear scattered across all three lanes
Some coins float at normal height — just run through them
Some coins are at elevated height — you need to jump or use Bean Power
Collect coins quickly in succession to build a COMBO MULTIPLIER
Hit 5x combo and earn bonus score points!
Coins earned during a run are added to your permanent bank when the run ends
Your coin bank never resets — keep saving up for that next character!
The game auto-saves every 10 seconds so you never lose progress

🎵 Sound Effects
All sounds in Subway Hoppers are procedurally generated using the Web Audio API — meaning there are no audio files to download! Every sound is created in real time:

🎵 Jump — A rising sine wave tone
🎵 Slide — A descending sawtooth sweep
🎵 Coin — A bright cheerful chime
🎵 Power-up — Three ascending musical notes
🎵 Crash — A deep rumbling descent
🎵 Game Start — An uplifting triangle wave fanfare
🎵 Purchase — A satisfying reward tone

You can toggle all sounds on/off with the 🔊 mute button at any time!
